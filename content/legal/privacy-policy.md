# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 4, 2025

---

## Overview

EliteSoft Technologies ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data necessary to deliver services and process transactions, including:

- **Contact Information:** Name, email, phone number, company name
- **Account Information:** Username, password, user profile data
- **Payment Information:** Billing address, payment method (processed securely via PCI-compliant payment gateways)
- **Service Data:** Project requirements, technical specifications, and communication records
- **Usage Data:** Log files, IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

- Deliver and improve our services
- Process transactions and send related confirmations
- Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
- Send marketing communications (with your consent)
- Comply with legal obligations
- Monitor and prevent fraud

---

## 3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

**We do not sell personal data.**

We may share data with:

- **Service providers** who assist in operations (payment processors, cloud hosts, analytics providers)
- **Legal authorities** when required by law or court order
- **Trusted partners** only with explicit consent

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
- Password hashing and secure storage
- Access controls and regular security audits
- Secure APIs and authentication

However, no method is 100% secure. Report security issues to [support@elitesoft.tech](mailto:support@elitesoft.tech).

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained while account is active; deleted upon request
- **Transaction records:** Retained for 7 years for compliance
- **Support tickets:** Retained for 2 years
- **Marketing emails:** Retained until unsubscribe

---

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion (subject to legal requirements)
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Data portability (receive a copy of your data)

**To exercise these rights:** Contact [support@elitesoft.tech](mailto:support@elitesoft.tech)

---

## 7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Remember login information
- Analyze usage patterns
- Improve user experience
- Serve personalized ads

You can control cookies in your browser settings.

---

## 8. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party sites. We're not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before providing information.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such data, we'll delete it immediately.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes become effective upon posting. We'll notify users of material changes via email.
